# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. lmapp is dedicated to giving everyone an equal chance to contribute regardless of gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, personal appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, age, religion, nationality, or level of experience.

---

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

- **Being respectful** of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- **Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism**
- **Focusing on what is best** for the community
- **Showing empathy** towards other community members
- **Being inclusive** and welcoming to newcomers
- **Helping others learn** and grow in their coding journey

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- **Harassment or discrimination** based on any protected characteristic
- **Offensive comments** related to gender, gender identity, sexual orientation, disability, mental illness, neuro(a)typicality, physical appearance, body size, race, or religion
- **Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following**
- **Harassing photography or recording** without consent
- **Sustained disruption** of community spaces (online or in-person)
- **Unwelcome sexual attention or advances**
- **Deliberate "outing" or misgendering** of individuals
- **Violence, threats of violence, or violent language** directed against another person
- **Advocating for, or encouraging, any of the above behavior**

---

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying the standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that violates the Code of Conduct.

Community leaders have the right and responsibility to:
- Remove comments, commits, code, issues, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct
- Temporarily or permanently ban any contributor for behaviors that violate this Code of Conduct

---

## Reporting Issues

**Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by:**

1. **Opening a GitHub Issue** (for non-urgent matters)
   - Use the title: `[Code of Conduct] Brief Description`
   - Include context and any relevant details

2. **Direct Email** (for sensitive matters)
   - Contact the maintainer directly if the issue involves the maintainer
   - Include: what happened, when, and any evidence

3. **Anonymous Report** (if preferred)
   - Use GitHub's community reporting feature
   - Provide as much context as possible

**All reports will be:**
- Treated confidentially
- Investigated promptly and fairly
- Handled by people who are not involved in the reported incident

---

## Consequences

Depending on the severity and pattern of behavior, consequences may include:

1. **Private warning** with explanation of why behavior was unacceptable
2. **Temporary suspension** from community spaces (3-30 days)
3. **Permanent ban** from contributing or participating
4. **Legal action** for severe violations or criminal behavior

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All community spaces associated with lmapp (GitHub, discussions, issues, pull requests)
- Interactions between community members
- Participation in lmapp events or activities
- Private communications if they relate to lmapp business

This Code of Conduct does NOT apply to:
- Private conversations unrelated to the project
- Discussions outside lmapp community spaces
